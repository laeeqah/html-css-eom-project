FAQ

    Do you pay?

    If you live in South Africa you don't need to pay but you can download it. If you live overseas you have to pay.

    Can you get the physical book at a book store?

    Yes you can, you can go to Simply Anime that is in Canalwalk.

    Do you deliver books?

    Yes we do. But only deliver by selected places. Like Olympic Town and District 100.

    Do you sell more than one genres?

    Yes we have a Variety of genres to please the customers.

    How will we know when our books are arriving?

    We will send you an email saying when, what time and place it will be arriving.
